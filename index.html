About Me

Turning Data Into Decisions

Passionate about leveraging data analytics to solve complex business problems

I am a results-driven Business Intelligence Developer at American & Efird Bangladesh Limited, where I leverage data analytics and visualization to drive strategic decision-making. Through advanced analysis and actionable insights, I have contributed to a 17% increase in sales, demonstrating the tangible business impact of data-driven strategies.

With a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Pabna University of Science and Technology and a Postgraduate Diploma in Data Science & Business Analytics, I combine strong technical expertise with a deep understanding of business operations.

My core expertise includes developing end-to-end BI solutions using Power BI, DAX, Power Query, SQL, Python, Data Analysis, Data Visualization and Azure. I specialize in designing robust data models, building scalable data warehousing solutions, and creating interactive dashboards that transform complex datasets into clear, meaningful insights for stakeholders at all levels.

Previously, at Apsis Solutions Ltd and Daraz Bangladesh (Alibaba Group), I gained extensive experience in dashboard development, advanced SQL querying, automated reporting systems, and cross-functional collaboration. I thrive in environments where data meets strategy, and I am passionate about continuous learning and staying aligned with the evolving landscape of data analytics and business intelligence.
